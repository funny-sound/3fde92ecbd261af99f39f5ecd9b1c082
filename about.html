Who we are

Đăng Khang Tech is a small but passionate mobile development studio based in Hanoi, Vietnam. We focus on creating high-quality mobile applications that solve real problems and improve daily life.

  • Mobile app development for Android and iOS (native and cross-platform)
  • Product-first approach: we prioritize user value and simple, delightful experiences
  • Performance-oriented engineering and pragmatic architecture
  • Ongoing maintenance, analytics, and feature iteration

Whether you need a full product team or targeted engineering help, we partner closely with you to deliver solutions that are reliable, maintainable, and loved by users.
