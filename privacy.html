Privacy Policy

App: Funny Sound • Android

1. Introduction

Welcome to Funny Sound. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it when you use the Funny Sound app on Android devices.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information: device model, Android version, device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), language, time zone and system settings to improve compatibility and performance.
  • Usage data: how you use the app, screens viewed, frequency of use, and crash reports to enhance the app experience and fix issues.
  • User preferences: settings you choose within the app (for example, sound on/off, ad preferences).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for purposes including:

  • Maintaining and improving the app.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to optimize user experience.
  • Delivering and measuring advertisements (if ads are enabled).
  • Providing technical support when you report issues.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share aggregated or anonymized data with analytics partners and third-party service providers to support app operations (for example, crash reporting services, analytics platforms, or ad networks). These partners are only permitted to use the data to provide services to us and must follow appropriate security standards.

5. Advertising and Third Parties

If the app displays ads, third-party ad providers may collect information (such as Advertising ID) to deliver relevant ads. You can manage ad tracking settings on your device (for example, resetting or limiting ad tracking) or via any in-app controls we provide.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure—if a material security breach occurs, we will work to address it and notify affected users when required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law. To make a request, please contact us using the contact information below.

8. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can investigate and delete the information if necessary.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and take effect on the date they are posted. Please review this policy periodically for updates.

10. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please email: itbeegroup@gmail.com
